Our Mission

Mission statement:
1. To be part of the church Jesus is building
2. To build men through discipleship networking
3. To pioneer centers across the globe wherever God will be relocating our disciples
4. To build and launch out young men and women who have identified their ministerial calling
5. To bring kingdom principles into the realities of every age and technological advancement
6. To be an instrument through which God will come down again into His church in a global revival

Our Vision

In the book of Acts, the local Church met in people's houses and other places convenient for the people. Synagogue was just a place to gather on Sunday to celebrate the risen Lord and because it would be open for public use on the same day as Jews worship on Saturday. The disciples did not need to worry about a physical building before starting a church, because wherever they gathered for fellowship was regarded as the church. With this system, the church grew rapidly because the people are considered as the church not the building. The people I believed built the buildings they needed in different cities as they grew in number and in the knowledge of God. READ MORE
