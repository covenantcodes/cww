A Letter to Our Faith Partners

Dear Sir/Ma,

“We started the vision of discipleship officially in the year 2016 when we were reintroduced to church growth through discipleship. Through the inspiration of the Holy Spirit, we named our fast growing cell network Church Without Walls, as an identity within NCC. We believe nothing would limit our spread to any part of the world God would send us and we wouldn't have boundaries because of the influx of people God will be adding to us (Zechariah 2:4-5 paraphrased). We have raised generations of youths who are now young adults, leading churches all over and we are still raising more. This move led to starting a youth centre in Elebele, Bayelsa state in 2018, another in Benin in the year 2022 and lately another at Otuoke, also in Bayelsa state last year October, 2023. We are soon taking over a centre in Elebele, Bayelsa state by the end of this month - May. All these centres are being led / pastored by young men raised through our discipleship network.
We intend to start our model centre sooner within Oluyole estate and Ring road environs, hoping to capture business minded and working class youth / young adults whose hearts yearn for the Lord. We trust God to spread across the globe from there. Since this group comprises majority of young people, those centres will automatically be pastored by such age groups, giving them an atmosphere where each would thrive in career, home, ministry and spiritual life. So we are encouraging young ones whom God has called into ministry to start their God given vision now, not waiting and hoping for someone's retirement before beginning what vision they have conceived. God gave us a word for this in 1 Chronicles 12:14; in fact the entire chapter is dedicated to the vision of the cell.
In fulfilling this vision, we would need financial structure that would work, which would put us in a position where we wouldn't need to beg for money when we want to pioneer or whatever project the ministry will be embarking upon, as we already have a series of activities lined up monthly through all our social media handles @nccchurchwithoutwalls. In view of this, we believe it's necessary we have a group of burden carriers that would be called "Faith Partners of Church Without Walls", who will have a monthly contribution sent to the account details of this group given below. For accountability, the account is run by trusted disciples. Newsletters containing achievements of the group would be posted from time to time on the platform for the partners so as to carry them along on progress and other activities.
Sincerely, this vision is big and its success is important to NCC Nigeria, global and to the church of Jesus as a whole. We need volunteers whom God has touched their hearts and also their pockets as well, who would like to give monthly commitments from now on (both from Nigeria and abroad), kindly click the link below and fill the partners form and you can be added to our partners group. Join Faith Partners ”
